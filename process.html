Our Process

From a spark of an idea to a fully realized structure, our process is a collaborative journey. We combine rigorous analysis with creative exploration to deliver exceptional results.

01. Discovery & Vision

We begin by listening. This initial phase is dedicated to understanding your goals, the site's context, and the project's potential. We conduct in-depth research and collaborative workshops to establish a clear, shared vision.

02. Schematic Design

Ideas take form as we translate the vision into initial design concepts. Through sketches, 3D models, and diagrams, we explore multiple possibilities, evaluating them for feasibility, aesthetics, and function.

03. Design Development

The chosen concept is refined. We develop detailed drawings, select materials, and integrate structural and mechanical systems. This phase solidifies the design and prepares it for technical documentation.

04. Construction Documentation

Precision is paramount. We create a comprehensive set of construction documents that serve as the definitive guide for contractors, ensuring every detail of the design is accurately translated into reality.

05. Construction & Finalization

We remain your dedicated partner throughout construction, overseeing the process to ensure quality, adherence to the design, and a seamless final handover. Your vision, meticulously realized.
